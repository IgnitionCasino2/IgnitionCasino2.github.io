Big four is hurt

Posted on | April 21, 2010 | No Comments

After losing some important players from the first eleven such as Adebayor or Toure, Arsenal are getting away  from the title that Wenger is waiting for since 2004. The speculations regarding Fabregas transfer are also a major concern for “The Gunners” manager. City are ready to offer €57 millions for him but there are also other teams interested.

Besides all these Tottenham showed them that they deserve to play in Europa League by winning against them on White Hart. With this victory Tottenham not only destroyed any title hope for Arsenal, considering the heavy games left but also the injuries, but also distance themselves 5 points ahead sixth place occupied by Liverpool.

Manchester United look like a tired old team and considering Ferguson declarations most certainly there will be some changes in the summer.

City remains on fourth place which ensures a place in Champions League qualifications.

At Liverpool everything seems to go wrong. After some less inspired transfers, Benitez had the misfortune of having the best players, Gerard and Torres, injured for a pretty long period of time.

If Aston Villa is sure of his place in Europa League because of the participation in League Cup Final, Liverpool is in danger of loosing it. Portsmouth also despite being already relegated have chances of playing in Europa League after playing in FA Cup Final. Anyway they have some big problems with the finances and it’s not sure if their request of playing in European Cups will be granted. In this case the only chance for Liverpool is by winning the Europa League.

Ancelotti seems to have a good season at Chelsea even from his first season at Stamford Bridge and looks like a coach able to keep his team on top of the Premier League. Chelsea are also in the FA CUp final were they will meet Portsmouth.

If in England everything goes well in Champions League the results were not so good, where they were eliminated by Inter.
